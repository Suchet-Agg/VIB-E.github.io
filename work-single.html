Introducing AIR VIB-E

Introducing NIKE VIB-E. These are the concept shoes created by team Croyant which connects the comfort with the next level of wearability. First of all, we have worked hard on the designing of the shoes and for this, we have added LEDs to the sole so that the visibility of shoes can be increased as well as it looks stunning.

Obstacle-E

is one of the vision features of this product which is comprised of the Advanced sensors which detects any possible obstacle in your path and vibrates the shoes and saves you from falling down. Cause you are a born WINNER! and never falls DOWN.

NOTI-E

is also one of the smart feature, the next-generation notification system, when you receive any sort of text and call on your phone it automatically vibrates the shoe in a particular manner so that you can be aware of any notification which comes on your phone without looking at your phone, it has a particular rhythm like shoe vibrates once for text messages and twice for calls.

WALK-E

is a revolutionary feature which connects to our product app where you can set your goals of walking and as soon as you completed your goal, It vibrates 4 times to let you know about your achievement that you have completed your goal today.

SAFETY-E

is a feature which is built to help people when they are in need, If you have met an accident you can click this SAFETY-E button and it will automatically call the person whom you have set as default. If you are women and you are struck in any adverse situation, click this button on your shoe and it automatically calls police if you have saved police number in your settings.
